## India's Current Situation on Governance

A look at the major developments shaping India this week.

## Economic Updates

### RBI Repo Rate Cut
The Reserve Bank of India has cut the repo rate to **6%**, signaling efforts to boost economic growth and make borrowing cheaper for businesses and consumers.

### Per-Capita Growth Concerns
Despite overall GDP growth, **per-capita growth** remains a concern. The benefits of economic expansion aren't reaching all sections of society equally.

## International Trade

### Trump Trade War Preparations
With ongoing global trade tensions, India is preparing for potential impacts from the Trump administration's trade policies. Diversification and self-reliance are becoming key focus areas.

## Security Situation

### Pahalgam Terrorist Attack
A tragic terrorist attack in **Pahalgam** has once again highlighted the security challenges in the region. The incident has sparked national outrage and calls for stronger security measures.

## Indo-Pak Relations

### Water Treaty Discussions
The **Indus Water Treaty** continues to be a point of discussion between India and Pakistan. Any changes to this decades-old agreement could have significant implications for both nations.

### Pakistan Chief Army Officer Statement
Recent statements from Pakistan's Chief of Army Staff have added to the diplomatic tensions. The rhetoric from both sides remains heated.

## Media Coverage

### BBC Portrayal Criticism
The **BBC's portrayal** of India has come under criticism from various quarters. The debate around international media's coverage of Indian affairs continues to be a contentious topic.
